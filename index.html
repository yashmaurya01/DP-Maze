About Differential Privacy

Differential Privacy (DP) adds "noise" or uncertainty to data to protect individual privacy. In this maze, a lower epsilon means more privacy but a fuzzier, more distorted maze, making it harder to navigate. A higher epsilon means less privacy but a clearer maze.

Adjust the epsilon slider to see how the maze changes in real-time.
